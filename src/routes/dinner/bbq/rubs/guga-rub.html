Guga Rub

Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
Instructions
  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Store in an airtight container.
  3. Use as a dry rub for your favorite meats.
