Sourdough Pancakes

Ingredients
  • 2 cups activated sourdough starter
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg, room temp
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp warm water
Instructions
  1. Stir starter, sugar, egg, olive oil, and salt.
  2. Mix warm water and baking soda.
  3. When pan is hot, fold in baking soda and water.
